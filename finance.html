What is IB?

The Engine of Global Finance

Investment bankers advise companies and governments on their most important financial decisions: mergers, acquisitions, IPOs and debt issuance. At the top firms in Amsterdam, you work on transactions worth hundreds of millions.

The work is demanding, long hours, steep learning curve, but the compensation and career acceleration are unmatched.
