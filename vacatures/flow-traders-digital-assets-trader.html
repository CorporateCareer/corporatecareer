Digital Assets Trader

Finance Permanent Amsterdam

Flow Traders is looking for a Digital Assets Trader for its Amsterdam office. This is a role in finance. Below you can read what the role involves and what you bring; you apply directly via Flow Traders's official job page.

What you will do

  • Trade digital assets and crypto-related products on international exchanges
  • Help develop and improve trading strategies and pricing models
  • Monitor markets and risk in real time and make decisions under time pressure

What we are looking for

  • A strong academic record in a quantitative field (econometrics, mathematics, physics, computer science)
  • Excellent numerical insight and fast decision-making
  • Excellent command of English

About Flow Traders

Flow Traders is a listed market maker in ETFs and other financial products, founded in Amsterdam and active on exchanges worldwide.
