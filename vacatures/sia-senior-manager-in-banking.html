Senior Manager in Banking

Finance Permanent Amsterdam

Sia is looking for a Senior Manager in Banking for its Amsterdam office. This is a role in finance. Below you can read what the role involves and what you bring; you apply directly via Sia's official job page.

What you will do

  • Lead advisory projects for banks on strategy, risk, regulation and transformation
  • Analyse complex questions and develop solutions
  • Present recommendations to client management

What we are looking for

  • A master's degree in an economic, financial or analytical field
  • Experience in or a strong affinity with financial services
  • Excellent command of Dutch and English

About Sia

Sia is an international management consultancy with more than 3,000 consultants worldwide and an office in Amsterdam, particularly strong in financial services.
