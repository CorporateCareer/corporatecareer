Manager Financial Due Diligence

Finance Permanent Amsterdam

PwC is looking for a Manager Financial Due Diligence for its Amsterdam office. This is a role in finance. Below you can read what the role involves and what you bring; you apply directly via PwC's official job page.

What you will do

  • Perform financial or commercial due diligence on acquisitions and investments
  • Analyse company figures, business plans and valuations
  • Report findings to private equity and corporate clients

What we are looking for

  • A master's degree in economics, finance, econometrics or accountancy
  • Experience in transaction advisory, audit or corporate finance
  • Strong analytical skills and excellent English

About PwC

PwC is one of the Big Four, providing advisory, audit and tax services. In the Netherlands it employs thousands of professionals, with a large office on the Zuidas in Amsterdam.
